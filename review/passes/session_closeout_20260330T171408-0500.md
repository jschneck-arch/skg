## Findings

- The AI gravity path has a concrete active-path defect:
  - `_exec_ai_probe()` passes `events_file` into `ai_probe.probe_device()`

  - `_exec_ai_probe()` then rewrites `events_file` from those summary dicts and projects the malformed file

- The auxiliary binary adapters remain comparatively clean structurally: `capa_analysis`, `angr_symbolic`, and `frida_trace` use the normal envelope and programmatic `run()` entry points, and gravity calls them through one generic `_exec_toolchain_adapter(...)` path.
