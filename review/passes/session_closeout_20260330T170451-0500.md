## Findings

- All three metacognition adapters emit `obs.substrate.node` records with a nonstandard envelope shape: no top-level `id`, `source.id` rather than `source_id`, and `evidence_rank` nested under `provenance.evidence`.
- The metacognition projector is history-sticky rather than recency-based: `blocked > realized > unknown` priority wins even when the blocked evidence is older.
- The web helper surface continues the semantic `WB-*` split beyond the main collector:
  - `auth_scanner` maps `WB-08`, `WB-09`, `WB-11`, and `WB-14` to meanings that do not match the checked-in catalog.
  - `gobuster_adapter` maps `WB-03`, `WB-04`, `WB-15`, `WB-17`, and `WB-20` to different meanings than the checked-in catalog.
  - `nikto_adapter` reuses several checked-in `WB-*` ids for different findings again.
- `gobuster_adapter.py`, `nikto_adapter.py`, and `sqlmap_adapter.py` emit manual event dicts without the shared `source` / `provenance` envelope, and some of those helpers use full URL strings inside `workload_id`.
- `sqlmap_adapter.py` also emits `DP-10` / `DP-02` data-domain events from the web helper path.
